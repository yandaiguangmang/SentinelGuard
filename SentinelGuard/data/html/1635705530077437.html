/vɑl ˈ keɪnoʊ/
noun
plural volcanoes or volcanos
Learner's definition of VOLCANO
[ count ]
: a mountain with a hole in the top or side that sometimes sends out rocks, ash, lava, etc., in a sudden explosion (called an eruption)
  • The volcano last erupted 25 years ago.
  • an active volcano [=a volcano that could erupt at any time or that is erupting now]
  • a dormant volcano [=a volcano that is not currently active]
  • an extinct volcano [=a volcano that does not erupt anymore]
