DVD Storage Ideas That Aren't an Eyesore

Choose a slide

Do you have a stash of DVDs that you reach for on a rainy day? If it's too difficult to let go of them, have no shame! There are definitely rom-coms and classics that you simply can't find on Netflix, and sometimes, it's too much of a hassle to make it to Redbox. But if you're at a loss when it comes to storing your DVDs, here are a few helpful ideas.

  • 1 of 1

    Try a Super Compact Solution

    Buy a paper towel rod (either vertical or horizontal for the wall) to store your DVDs -- just slip the DVDs onto the towel rod! This method requires a bit more effort when it comes time to search for that perfect film for the night -- or it could be a serendipitous game of chance as to which film you view.
