Welcome to Dar-us-Salam Islamic Bookstore!

We offer books in English, Arabic, Urdu, Spanish and many other languages of the world - all according to Quran & Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad (S). We also carry CDs & DVDs, children toys, Islamic clothing, prayer caps & rugs, azan clocks, decorations, gifts and more. Order online 24/7 and we will promptly ship your order next business day. Insha-Allah. We ship worldwide from our store in Houston, TX USA which we welcome you to visit. Our head office is based in Saudi Arabia with outlets throughout the world.

Due to COVID-19 USPS delivery is temporarily suspended to these countries .
