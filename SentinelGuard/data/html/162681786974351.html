Description of Pay with Yandex.Money

Add money to your mobile phone, travel pass, and gaming account balances. Transfer money to your mom's card or friend's wallet. Pay for the Internet and utility bills. Accepting bank cards issued by Russian banks and Yandex.Money wallets.

—————————

FULL LIST OF APP'S FEATURES

Most important payments

— MTS, Beeline, Megafon, Tele2, and other telecom service providers,

— Internet services from various providers,

— utilities (including receipts containing QR-codes),

— Steam, World of Tanks, and other games,

— VK.com and Odnoklassniki balances,

— loans from any Russian bank

Money transfers

— between wallets (no commission),

— from card to card,

— between a card and a wallet.

Contactless card

The app includes a contactless card—pay from the wallet in supermarkets, cafes, and subway rail stations by tapping your device against the sales register. Important: if you want the contactless card to work properly, your device should support NFC, and you need to be authorized in the app.

Discounts and bonuses

This section contains new discounts, cashbacks and other gifts selected specially for you—with updates almost every day. Today, it's a free cup of coffee, tomorrow, it's a discount for airline tickets. You can save the discounts you like to use them later, just don't forget to regularly check the section. You wouldn't want to miss something important!

Chat with Support Service

Ask a quesiton about Yandex.Money in our chat, and you will get a response within a few minutes. Open the chat from the app's main menu.

Manage wallet and cards

In the app, you can:

— check and replenish the wallet's balance from a card (no commission),

— find nearest ATMs to withdraw money,

— set up important payments and autopayments,

— check the payment history for cards and wallet,

— change the PIN-code of a Yandex.Money card, inform us of an upcoming trip, or cancel the card.
